Welcome

Welcome to Coming Within℠.

This Sovereign Spark℠ Practice invites you to notice where you may have learned to leave yourself in order to be accepted, approved of, protected, or included.

There is no pressure to force a breakthrough or find a perfect answer. The goal is simply to notice, reflect, and practice one small return to yourself.

Coming Within℠ is a short self-reflection practice designed to help you notice where you may have learned to hide, please, shrink, perform, or trade authenticity for approval.

This practice is Pride-rooted but universal. It may speak to LGBTQ+ experiences of hiding, code-switching, and self-protection, while also supporting anyone who has learned to leave themselves in order to stay accepted, safe, or connected.

Practice objective: Notice one place where you may be living from outside approval instead of inner truth, and choose one small way to return to yourself.

Lesson 1

The Self You Had to Hide

Explore where you may have learned to hide, edit, shrink, or soften parts of yourself to stay connected or safe.

Where did I learn that being fully myself might cost me connection?

Lesson 2

The Approval Costume

Notice the roles you may wear to stay acceptable, wanted, safe, or unthreatening.

What version of me shows up when I am trying not to disappoint, upset, or lose someone?

Lesson 3

One Small Return

Practice one small way to come back to yourself without forcing a dramatic reinvention.
